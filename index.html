The Hunger Games

Author: Suzanne Collins

Genre: Dystopian / Science Fiction

Year Released: 2008

Awards: California Young Reader Medal, Publishers Weekly’s Best Book of the Year
Fun Fact: The book was inspired by flipping TV channels between reality shows and war coverage!

The Hunger Games is a thrilling dystopian novel set in the nation of Panem, where twelve districts are ruled by the oppressive Capitol.
Each year, young people are forced to participate in a deadly televised competition known as the Hunger Games.
The story follows Katniss Everdeen, who volunteers to take her sister’s place in the Games.
It’s a gripping tale of survival, sacrifice, and rebellion against injustice.
